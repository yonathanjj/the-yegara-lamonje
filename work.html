Actionaid Ethiopia

La Monje partnered with AAE to leverage our expertise in communication design and branding. We co-created high-quality communication materials that effectively amplify AAE's work and drive positive change. Our team provided strategic guidance and collaborated closely with AAE to ensure all materials aligned with their communication and branding guidelines.



LookUp Workshop

LookUp, a La Monje creation, is a thriving monthly event series in Hawassa, Ethiopia. Designed to foster connections and collaboration between youth entrepreneurs, LookUp brings together ambitious entrepreneurs to create a wide network of youth entrepreneurs, a marketplace for emerging businesses, and a platform to connect employees with employers.


PSY Addis

We had the pleasure to co-organize PSY Addis, a pulsating monthly music festival in Addis Ababa, alongside Aslan Business Group. This project fostered a thriving community around music, nature, and connection through psychedelic and electronic beats. We collaborated closely to curate a wild experience, engaging marketing campaign, and ticket sales oversight.


Mignot Bazzar

La Monje partnered with Eykis Entertainment to create a monthly bazaar series. This dynamic marketplace provides a platform for business owners to showcase their products, connect with customers, and drive sales. We collaborated to facilitate and manage the event, for sponsorship acquisition, vendor registration, content creation, and event day photography.


Youth-Employment Policy Workshop

La Monje was entrusted by PEP and MCF to translate their research on youth employability in Ethiopia into action. Through a series of impactful events (training, workshops, panel discussions, research presentations), we fostered collaboration and knowledge sharing among stakeholders.
